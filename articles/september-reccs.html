This is a list of all the new music I've listened to and liked over the last month that I think I should share. They'r arranged in no particular order, other than maybe the order in which they show up in my Spotify library.


(SoundCloud) Lindsay Bronnenkant - Tarot, II. The King of Cups
This whole suite is phenomenal, but this second movement is probably my favorite. I played this my senior year of high school and that was a time when my compositional skill was reaching an low-adolescent stage. The theme (Gb-F-Gb-Bb-F-Ab-Gb) was something particularly beautiful to me, and I've used in many of my own pieces before, especially in trombones, whose statement of it I just find so particularly regal, yet sensitive, and so broad no matter its dynamic.
