I have seen hell. It is not the fire and brimstone of now and the past, or the evil rivers of the ancients, no. Hell, death's realm, is the simplest place that could ever be. There is no topology, no watershed, no levels of ice and flame. It is as simple as nothing. Hell, you see, is a shapeless void where you, your soul, that of a sinner, is left simply to be. Because what greater torture is there, after one experiences their final moments, the sweet release of death, than to be?
