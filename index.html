Welcome!

My name is Wrzeczak (not my real name, of course), and this is my website. I am a Pole, born and raised in Georgia and later Texas. The name is a made-up word meant to sound Polish, loosely based off "dresiarz." I am a writer of music, articles, and code. Bass trombone is my main instrument, but I have faculties on the piano and a few other brass instruments. I program small games and other bits and pieces of code, as a mental exercise and hobby more than anything else. My father first taught me to program around first grade, so it's been a lifelong engagement.
I'm from a Polish Catholic background, but have recently been deeply interested in Orthodox theology as well, and in the consideration of a conversion. I'm a Christian libertarian leftist, and as such not a Marxian. My primary personal influences are Nietzsche, Kierkegaard, Fr. Seraphim Rose, Dostoyevsky, and Camus.

Special thanks to envis10n for hosting poop.uno and wrzeczak.net for over a year, all for free. His help and generosity in getting my website off the ground cannot be overstated.

If you ever need to contact me, you can email me at wrzeczak@protonmail.com (custom mail server coming soon?), or more immediately on Discord as wrzeczak. My GitHub can be found here.
